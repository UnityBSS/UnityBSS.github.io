UNT-FC.1

Trade Display

While the trade display will not be made this year due to the unfortunate circumstances of Covod-19. It was of upmost importance to model it 100% accurate as to what we would produce if we were to make it. To do this we utilised an advanced frame that we called the ‘skeleton’. The skeleton is made of a number of aluminium metal rods with 3D-Printed joins to hold it all together and make it easy to assemble.

One of the main features of the trade display was the car, we wanted to allow anyone visiting the trade display to get a great understanding of our car and the engineering behind it. To do this we developed an advanced mechanical system discretely hidden beneath the table. This system clevely allows the car to rotate 360º, explode the wheel system, front wing and tether line guide, as well as raise the car body up and off the table surface.
