Meet the Team

Luke Gurney

Engineering / Manufacture

As team manager, it is Luke’s job to coordinate the team, as well as organise meetings and ensure everything is completed on time. As engineer and manufacturer, Luke will design prototypes and final car designs and ready those designs to be 3D printed, CNC milled, painted and decalled, then hand submit the team’s work for judging.

Bayan Maghsoodi

Marketing / Industry Collaborations

As the marketing manager, it is Bayan’s responsibility to find and reach out to sponsors and smaller industries/companies to seek monetary support for the team. Bayan will also reach out to places offering product sponsorship to collect resources or mentorship for the team.

Reilly Bennet

Display Engineer / Graphic Designer

Reilly’s main role is to focus on designing the team’s trade display, as well as creating the team’s graphics. He is responsible for most of the manufacture of our trade display. Reilly also assists with running the team with prior experience in competition from the 2019 state finals, suggesting ideas where possible.

Sydney Martin

Marketing / Media Manager

Sydney’s main job in the team is to work with Bayan to contact and form potential sponsorships as well as for monetary support for the team. Sydney is also in charge of running and posting on our social media pages, ensuring that our sponsors, partners and supporters are all kept up to date as we progress.

Douglas Cooke

Programming / Electronics

Douglas's main job is to make sure the team is a step ahead of its competition by developing innovative new ways of displaying the car and its features. To do this, Douglas works closely with Reilly to bring technology into the pit display, and to make sure the website is the best it can be.
